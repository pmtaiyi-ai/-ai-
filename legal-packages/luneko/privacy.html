Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026 · Last Updated: April 14, 2026 · Contact: phanvannhieu8589@icloud.com

1) Overview

Luneko (“we”, “our”, “the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and what choices you have.

2) Information We Collect

A. Information you provide

  • Account identifiers if you choose to sign in (for example, Apple ID’s unique identifier and profile basics provided by the login service).
  • Content you create in the App (notes, entries, labels, preferences, and settings).
  • Optional details you enter (for example, city or time zone used for localized reminders).

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device and app details (model, OS version, app version), network status, and crash/error logs for stability.
  • Aggregated usage data (feature frequency, flows) that is not directly linked to your identity.
  • Notification delivery status (sent / delivered). We do not track whether you click a notification.

C. Permissions (optional)

  • Notifications: reminders and alerts you enable.
  • Photos/Camera: attaching images you choose to import or capture.
  • Microphone: voice notes if you choose to use them.

3) How We Use Information

  • Provide core functions (create, view, and manage your entries).
  • Generate summaries and insights based on your inputs (for example, preference trends).
  • Maintain reliability (debugging, performance improvement, preventing abuse).

4) Storage, Retention, and Security

  • Local-first: by default, your content is stored on your device.
  • Optional sync/backup: if you enable it, your data may be uploaded to a cloud provider you authenticate with or services we operate. Transmission is protected by TLS.
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational measures such as access controls and encryption in transit; no method is 100% secure.

5) Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information for advertising.
  • We may share minimal data with service providers only to run the App (e.g., hosting or crash analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

6) Your Controls

  • Access and correction: edit most information in the App.
  • Delete: remove entries or request deletion of synced backups by contacting us.
  • Export: export your data for archiving if the App provides an export option.
  • Permissions: manage notifications, camera, photos, and microphone in system settings.

7) Children

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Updates

We may update this policy. The “Last Updated” date will change, and significant updates may be shown in-app.
