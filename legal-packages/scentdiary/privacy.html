Summary

This policy describes how ScentDiary collects and uses information. We aim to collect the minimum needed to run the App.

Information Categories

  • Journal content: entries, tags, and preferences you save; photos you choose to attach.
  • Account basics (optional): if you sign in, a unique identifier and limited profile basics from the login provider.
  • Device diagnostics: device model, OS/app version, network status, crash logs.
  • Usage metrics: aggregated feature usage to improve experience, not directly identifying you.

Why We Use It

  • To store and display your journal, and generate summaries or insights based on what you log.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • To protect against abuse and security issues.

Where Data Lives

  • Local-first storage on your device for privacy and offline use.
  • If you enable optional sync/backup, data is transferred using TLS and stored with reasonable access controls.

Sharing

  • No selling or renting personal information for advertising.
  • Limited sharing with vendors to operate the service (hosting, diagnostics) under confidentiality terms.
  • Legal disclosure when required.

Your Rights

  • Edit, delete, and manage your entries inside the App.
  • Export data if export is available in the App.
  • Control permissions (notifications, photos/camera, microphone) in device settings.
  • Contact us for help deleting synced backups.
